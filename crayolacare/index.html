Crayolacare Hospital

The Crayolacare AU is a fanmade AU of Sparklecare focusing on a different hospital in Palentine that has started torturing it's patients ever since being bought out by Theda Incorporated.

This is the information page, containing stuff like Frequently Asked Questions, a list of arcs, etc.
